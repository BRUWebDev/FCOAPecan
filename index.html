About

Owner Portal

Fairway Condominiums is a 52-unit community within the gates of Pecan Plantation. These condominiums feature 1- and 2-bedroom homes with a private patio/balcony, and a full size swimming pool with a cabana, ideal for entertaining guests' cookouts, meetings and spending time with friends.

Experience the quiet life in our unique neighborhood, nestled among the trees, away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Take a drive in your golf cart to the activity center, walking trails, and shopping at the supermarket or one of the many businesses located right here in Pecan … watch out for the tame deer crossing the road!

Rules and Regulations

Important Rules to know before selling or leasing

  • Domestic house pets shall be permitted, provided they are housed within a condominium unit and are not bothersome to others. Limit two pets per unit with a maximum weight of 25 lbs. each.
  • Pets must be on a leash at all times when on common areas of FCOA property. Pets should not be tied, staked, or allowed to run free on any common areas of FCOA property.
  • Animal waste must be picked up by the owner or tenant or guest and properly disposed of in a plastic bag.
  • All vehicles parked on the property shall be operational. Any inoperable vehicle shall not be stored in a parking space or within the common elements in general. No storage of vehicles on property is permitted.
  • Patios or balconies are permitted to have the usual and customary outdoor furniture including gas or electric grills. Charcoal grills or fire pits (open flames) are not permitted.
  • No lifeguard is on duty, swim at your own risk.
  • Guests are to park in uncovered parking spots only.
